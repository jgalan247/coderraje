Internet of Things Solutions

Teaching IoT through hands-on hardware, modern protocols, and practical applications

Our Approach

At Coderra, we deliver IoT courses that combine theory with practice. Learners explore microcontrollers, sensors, and networking protocols such as MQTT and LoRa, while applying their knowledge through industry-standard software like Node-RED and the Arduino IDE. Each course is tailored on demand and designed for small audiences, ensuring maximum interaction and personalised learning. A baseline familiarity with programming is recommended, allowing participants to dive deeper into solving real-world IoT challenges.

What You'll Learn

Microcontrollers

Hands-on with Micro:bits, Raspberry Pi, and Pico to build IoT prototypes.

MQTT & LoRa

Publish, subscribe, and transmit data securely with MQTT and long-range IoT networks.

Software Tools

Create flows with Node-RED, develop with Arduino IDE, and integrate cloud services.

Tailored Learning

Small-group sessions crafted on demand, focusing on your goals and prior knowledge.

KS5 Student Applications

With advanced learners at Key Stage 5, our IoT courses move beyond theory into practical, impactful applications. Students work with Raspberry Pi, Pico, and a range of sensors to design and implement real-world systems, including:

Orchard Irrigation Control

Automated watering systems driven by soil-moisture sensors.

Water Quality Monitoring

Using turbidity and pH sensors to track and analyse local sources.

Air Quality Measurement

Deploying gas sensors for pollution monitoring.

Wind Speed Analysis

Capturing and visualising environmental data for energy and safety insights.

These projects not only reinforce programming and electronics, but also highlight the social and environmental relevance of IoT solutions.

About Me

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Registered Counselling Psychologist & Wellness Coach

Hello, I'm Dr. Sarah Mitchell, and I'm here to support you on your journey towards emotional wellbeing and personal growth. With over 15 years of experience in counselling psychology, I specialize in helping individuals navigate life's challenges with compassion and evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

My practice is grounded in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings freely. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, or simply seeking personal development, I'm committed to walking alongside you every step of the way.

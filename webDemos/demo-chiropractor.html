About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Welcome! I'm Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a dedicated chiropractor with over 12 years of experience helping people across Jersey live pain-free, healthier lives. I qualified from the Anglo-European College of Chiropractic in 2012 with a Master of Chiropractic degree and have been passionate about spinal health ever since.

My approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern evidence-based practices. I believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms, and work closely with each patient to create personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and goals.

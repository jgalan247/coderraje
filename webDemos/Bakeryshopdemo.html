Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Baked with Love

Welcome to Golden Crust Bakery, where tradition meets taste! For over 5 years, we've been Jersey's favorite neighborhood bakery, baking fresh artisan breads, pastries, and cakes every single day.

Founded by master baker Jean-Pierre Rousseau, our bakery combines time-honored French baking techniques with local Jersey ingredients to create exceptional baked goods that bring people together.

From our signature sourdough that takes 48 hours to prepare, to our buttery croissants made fresh every morning at 5am, every item that leaves our ovens is a testament to quality, craftsmanship, and care.

Our Vision

At Coderra, we believe Computer Science should be practical, inclusive, and inspiring. Our courses are designed with structured Schemes of Learning that align to the national curriculum, supported by online resources for learners of all ages and abilities. We use hands-on technology to bridge abstract concepts with real-world solutions, ensuring students gain both confidence and competence.

Key Stage 3

Foundations

  • Scheme of Learning

    Clear pathways introducing programming, logic, and problem-solving.

  • Micro:bit Projects

    Build and code interactive devices, linking sensors and outputs to real scenarios.

  • Physical Computing

    Use microcontrollers to show how abstract problems become tangible solutions.

  • Core Principles

    Programming basics, Boolean logic, modular design, and hardware/software integration.

Key Stage 4

Advancement

  • Advanced Programming

    Python projects using Raspberry Pi Pico for robotics, IoT, and data handling.

  • Artificial Intelligence

    Introduction to AI concepts and classroom-ready applications that reflect modern practice.

  • Online Resources

    Tailored materials to support students at every ability level.

  • Problem-Solving & Design

    Develop computational thinking for coursework and real-world challenges.

Key Stage 5

Expertise

  • Project-Based Learning

    Use Raspberry Pi and Pico to tackle complex, extended problems.

  • AI & Emerging Tech

    Explore machine learning, automation, and their ethical implications.

  • University Preparation

    Build the skills and knowledge to transition confidently into higher education or industry.

  • Mentorship

    Sessions delivered by a PhD Computer Scientist with examination expertise.
